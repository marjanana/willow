<p>Last week was not the best for me. Icky in a few ways. I kind of felt like Jessica&#39;s son felt (above). But on Sunday, I decided to start editing these photographs of Jessica and her family and they made me smile. I&#39;ve known Jessica for almost 10 years. I made a short documentary about her and <a href="http://www.willowpaule.com/blog/2014/12/hope-love-happiness-happy-holidays" target="_blank">her sister Darlenia</a> when they were teenagers. Now they&#39;re both mothers. Jessica has three lovely kids and a husband; and her teenage sister-in-law has just moved in with them because she is finishing high school here in the US (her parents live in Mexico).</p>
