I've been working hard on making my place in the Phoenix community, applying for grants, jobs and exhibits but for the last week, I've really been missing making photographs. Looking at photos and editing is an important part of the process of grant-writing for a photographer, but it's also really hard work. It doesn't help that the weather in Phoenix has been amazingly warm and sunny. Who wants to sit inside when the orange blossoms are coming out and their floral fragrance perfumes the air?<br/>
